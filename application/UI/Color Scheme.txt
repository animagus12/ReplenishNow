#### *1. Home Page*

     - Logo on the left
     - Navigation links (Home, About, Products, Contact) centered
     - User login/register link on the right
   - *Main Content*:
     - Banner image with a call-to-action button (e.g., "Subscribe Now!")
     - Featured products or subscription plans
     - Customer testimonials
   - *Footer*:
     - Links to privacy policy, terms of service, and social media icons
     - Contact information

   - *Color Scheme*:
     - *Background*: Light gray or white (#F5F5F5 or #FFFFFF)
